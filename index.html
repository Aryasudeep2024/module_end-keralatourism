Experience Kerala

Kollam

Experience the serene backwaters of Kerala. The canoe tour takes off in Monroe island.A quaint island located at the confluence of Ashtamudi Lake and the Kallada river at a distance of 25km from Kollam

Backwaters

The scenic backwaters of Kerala comprise serene stretches of lakes, canals and lagoons located parallel to the coast of the Arabian Sea. The backwater regions of Kerala are one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

Kayaking

Alleppey Backwater Village is one of the best places for kayaking in Kerala. It's a peaceful and beautiful place where you can paddle through calm waters

Waterfalls

Athirapilly is the largest waterfall in Kerala, which stands tall at 81.5 feet. Just a short drive from Athirapilly is the Vazhachal Falls.

Munnar-Rose garden

At an elevation of 3500 feet above sea level, the Rose Garden offers a cool and refreshing escape from the heat, making it a popular destination year-round.

Ponmudi

Ponmudi is a hill station in the Thiruvananthapuram district of Kerala, India. It's a popular destination for tourists, especially on weekends, and is known for its: Scenic beauty
